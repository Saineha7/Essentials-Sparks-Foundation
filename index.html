About Us

Our organisation Essentials runs entirely with public contributions from Individuals, Organizations, Trusts, Companies and Institutions etc. The resources of the Essentials relief fund are utilized to render immediate relief to families of those killed in natural calamities like floods, cyclones and earthquakes, etc. The funds will also be used to help farmers in need and those who have lost crops due to natural disasters.

Every single donor has the right to know where their donation goes.
